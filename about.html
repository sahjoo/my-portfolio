My great love for stories, art and technology drive me today.

From a young age, I loved beautiful art, great stories, and cool technology. My non-stereotypical asian parents encouraged me to read books and watch movies. I was so obsessed with Disney that I dressed up as Belle for Halloween until I could no longer fit into that yellow dress. I sketched Disney princesses and manga. I blame my passion for technology on my dad, an electrician and security enginer, who built computers and drones, and got me excited about tech too.

I hope to spark inspiration by telling great stories and help the world to become better by collaborating on meaningful projects.

Although I loved art, I switched direction to become a physician’s assistant to heal people. Along the way, someone dear to me got sick and I became a caretaker. This experience made me realize how short life can be. While working in the debt collection industry, I craved a meaningful job where I could practice design. As a caretaker, I tried to find games that could aid in recovery and mobility, but found few that fit specific needs. After immersing myself into an 8-month program where I developed visual design, user research and front development skills, I’m happy to be able to solve problems like above by paying attention to and listening to users’ needs.

What do I do in my spare time?

You can find me exploring new cafes and restaurants, reading novels or comics, listening to podcasts, traveling, stargazing, and chasing after sunsets!
